Getting Started


If you are reading this, that means you are interested in our using our system for your business,

Below given steps are a brief introduction on how to move forward from here,

Make sure to watch all videos or give us a call or hit us an email for further details.

Step 1: We set up an Account for you on our Servers

We set up a blank account for you on our servers this takes a few minutes to do. Once this is done and you are notified, you can start using the cloud app by logging in using the shared application URL with your unique user name and password. Then go to the People Menu to start adding your staff or customers and go to the Inventory Menu to start adding items with prices and pics.

Step 2: You give us an Excel Spreadsheet of your Customers

Instead of manually adding all your customers, we will import your customers for you so we will need them according to their zonal and field staff distribution along with their opening balances and opening stock. 

Step 3: Add your Staff, Zones and Items into the System                                    

Once we have imported your customers you can start assigning zones to the customers, simply go to Territory Menu, add the zone, find the customer and assign it to the zone. Add your field staff by simply going to the People Menu. Add items by going to the Inventory Menu. Finally schedule your zones and assign them to your field staff by going to the Schedule Menu. 

Step 4: Post Sales using the Rider App

Our support staff will provide the link to the rider app, the field staff will login using the credentials you have entered in the previous step. Customer list will appear according to the order in which they were either manually added or uploaded. The Rider can simply click on any customer, select items and post the bill.  

Step 5: Post Sales using the Cloud Application

You can also post customer sales using the cloud app by going to the Operations Menu, select the customer, select the item, select the staff, enter the amount received and post the bill.

Step 6: Going Live with Tarsil system 

We highly suggest and recommend that you use the system for 2 to 3 weeks before you go live and start posting your actual sales using the application, this is to ensure that you and your staff get a proper know how of the working of the system.
